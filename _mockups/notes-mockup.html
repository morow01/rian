Summary of options
Option A — Colour-border cards. Visually bold, easy to scan priority at a glance. Best for moderate number of notes.
Option B — Compact rows. Fits the most notes on screen. Good if you expect many notes. Filter chips let you sort by priority.
Option C — Expandable cards (like the timesheet day cards). Familiar feel, inline editing. Best if you want a consistent UX with the rest of the app.
Add/Edit form — The bottom sheet style works for all three options.
