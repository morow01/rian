What changed
✅ Shared top nav & tab bar — same dark navy header on all 3 screens, making them feel like one app

✅ Consistent card pattern — tasks and notes use the same card shell, expand gesture, and field layout

✅ Unified colour semantics — amber always means overtime/medium, red always means high/delete, green always means done/low

✅ Exchange Finder reframed as a tab — not a separate app, just the "Find" tab with a clean toggle for Exchanges vs Cabinets

✅ DM Sans + DM Mono — one typeface pair across all screens (mono for codes/hours, sans for everything else)

✅ Consistent add-item pattern — "+" dashed row at the bottom of every list
