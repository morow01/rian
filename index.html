01 — Timesheet

Track every hour, every day

Log your ordinary and overtime hours against work codes for each day of the week. Everything stays organised and ready to export.

  • Weekly overviewSee your full week at a glance — tap any day to expand and add tasks.
  • Ordinary & overtime hoursLog both hour types per task. Overtime is highlighted automatically.
  • Work codes & locationsAttach job codes and site locations to each task for accurate reporting.
  • Email & exportSend a formatted weekly summary directly from the app in one tap.
  • Cloud backup & snapshotsAuto-saved to Firestore with snapshot history — restore any point in time from Cloud Backups.
  • Desktop three-panel layoutOn screens wider than 1280px, the timesheet opens into Days → Tasks → Detail panels with a built-in Weekly Summary sidebar.

02 — Notes

Notes that work as hard as you do

Capture faults, follow-ups, meeting outcomes, and job reminders — all categorised, filtered, and searchable. Nothing falls through the cracks.

  • 6 colour-coded categories Job, Fault, Follow-up, Meeting, Personal, Idea — each with its own icon and colour so you know at a glance what every note is about.
  • Smart filter bar Filter by category and priority at the same time. Active filters show as dismissible pills in the collapsed header. High (4) · Med (5) · Low (3) · Done (6).
  • Reminders & push notifications Set a reminder on any note and get a push notification when it's due — even when the app is closed.
  • Voice dictation Hands full on site? Dictate your note directly. Speech-to-text fills the note field without you typing a word.
  • Search, bulk actions & custom tags Full-text search across all notes. Long-press to select multiples — mark done or delete in one go. Create your own tags with the Tag Manager.
  • Desktop editor panel On desktop, notes open with a sidebar (Active · Archive · Bin · Tags), a scrollable list, and a full editor pane — just like the mobile view but roomier.

03 — Journal New

Your digital field notebook

Organise site visits, voltage readings, ticket tables and photos into notebooks, sections and pages — structured the way you think, with rich text editing powered by TipTap.

  • Notebooks → Sections → Pages Three-level hierarchy mirrors how you organise work — one notebook per project, sections for areas, pages for individual visits.
  • Rich text editor Headings, bold, lists, tables, images, code blocks — a full TipTap editor so your field notes look professional.
  • Cloud sync Every page syncs to Firestore in real time. Start a note on your phone, finish it on your tablet — it's always up to date.
  • Colour-coded sections Assign colours to sections so you can tell areas apart at a glance. Copy or move pages between sections easily.
  • Voice-to-table recorder Tap the battery icon and dictate readings like "24 cells, cell 1 is 12.4 volts…" — the AI walks you through each value and inserts a formatted table straight into your page.

05 — Routines

Track every routine visit, month by month

See your full year of routine site visits in one grid. Choose the sites you maintain regularly and the app fills in each month automatically from your timesheet — no double entry.

  • Monthly visit gridSites as rows, months as columns — one glance tells you exactly what's been done and what's overdue.
  • Auto-detected from timesheetRoutine visits already on your timesheet are picked up automatically and marked with an AUTO badge.
  • One-tap navigationTap any site name to open Google Maps directions straight to the exchange or cabinet.
  • Manual log overrideVisit done but not on the timesheet? Log it manually against any month with a tap.
  • Stats, Due & Never VisitedMobile tab switcher (Grid · Stats · Due) shows visits per month, coverage, sites never visited, and sites overdue by 3+ months. Tap Open Day to jump straight to the timesheet entry.
  • Desktop dashboardOn desktop, Routines opens as a full dashboard — stats grid, monthly bar chart, Due Visits, Never Visited, and Recent Visits list, all side-by-side.

06 — Callout

Your on-call record, always accurate

Track every on-call week, log each incident against it, and see your stats build over time. Everything your supervisor or payroll team needs — organised by week, ready to share.

  • On-call scheduleMark which weeks you're on call or doing extra shifts so your callout history is always in context.
  • Log callout incidentsRecord each fault call against its week — time, site, fault description, and actions taken.
  • Stats at a glanceSee your total on-call weeks, extra shifts, incidents logged, and average incidents per week — all in one bar.
  • Included in weekly exportWhen you email your weekly timesheet, callout entries for that week are automatically included — no extra steps.
  • Paste TicketPaste a fault ticket straight from Remedy — site, fault, and date are parsed automatically and a new callout is created for you.
  • Desktop three-panelWeeks → Callouts → Detail layout on desktop, with stats panel and draft Save/Discard buttons for every new entry.

07 — Desktop Mode New

A full desktop layout, not just a big phone

When the screen is wide enough, Rian switches to a purpose-built desktop layout with dedicated panels for navigation, lists, and detail editing. No cramped phone UI stretched across a monitor.

  • Three-panel timesheetDays · Tasks · Detail — select a day on the left, a task in the middle, and edit description, hours, and work codes on the right.
  • Weekly Summary sidebarCollapsible summary with percentage bars and ORD/OT columns — always visible while you log hours. Weekend days auto-collapse when empty.
  • Every view reimaginedNotes, Callouts, Finder and Routines each get their own desktop layout — sidebar navigation, wide editors, and live Google Maps in Finder.
  • Synced with mobileSame data, same state — collapse a section on desktop and it's collapsed on your phone too. Universal ESC key closes whichever modal is open.
