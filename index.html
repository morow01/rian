02 — Notes Updated

Notes that work as hard as you do

Capture faults, follow-ups, meeting outcomes, and job reminders — all categorised, filtered, and searchable. Nothing falls through the cracks.

  • 6 colour-coded categories Job, Fault, Follow-up, Meeting, Personal, Idea — each with its own icon and colour so you know at a glance what every note is about.
  • Smart filter bar Filter by category and priority at the same time. Active filters show as dismissible pills in the collapsed header. High (4) · Med (5) · Low (3) · Done (6).
  • Reminders & push notifications Set a reminder on any note and get a push notification when it's due — even when the app is closed.
  • Voice dictation Hands full on site? Dictate your note directly. Speech-to-text fills the note field without you typing a word.
  • Search & bulk actions Full-text search across all notes. Long-press to select multiples — mark done or delete in one go.

Track every routine visit, month by month

See your full year of routine site visits in one grid. Choose the sites you maintain regularly and the app fills in each month automatically from your timesheet — no double entry.
