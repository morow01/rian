01Timesheet

Log hours against codes, day by day.

Tap a day, add a task, pick a work code, enter ordinary and overtime hours. The app totals your week, highlights overtime, and exports a formatted summary by email.

39hordinary +3overtime · this week

Week at a glance

All seven days in one scroll, with task counts and hours inline. Tap a day to expand and add tasks.

Ordinary and overtime, separately

Each task has ORD and OT fields. The weekly summary totals both by work code.

Searchable work codes

Type to search codes like KLE or MXY0002A10. Bundles insert several related codes in one go.

Weekly email export

Formatted summary with ORD/OT breakdown, codes and descriptions, sent from the app.

Snapshot history

Every save backs up to Firestore. Restore any week from Cloud Backups in the menu.

Desktop three-panel layout

On wide screens the timesheet opens into Days · Tasks · Detail with a Weekly Summary sidebar.

02Notes

Notes for everything that isn't a timesheet entry.

Faults, meetings, follow-ups, parts on order. Each note has a category, a priority and an optional reminder with a push notification.

Six built-in categories

Job, Fault, Follow-up, Meeting, Personal, Idea. Each with its own colour and icon. Add custom tags via the Tag Manager.

Filter by category and priority

Combine filters to narrow the list. Active filters show as dismissible pills above the notes.

Reminders with push notifications

Set a date and time on any note. The notification fires when it's due, even if the app is closed.

Dictation, search, bulk actions

Tap the mic to dictate. Full-text search across all notes. Long-press to multi-select and mark done or delete in one pass.

Desktop list grouping

On desktop the notes list groups into Overdue, Pinned and Open bands. Convert to Task and Add to Calendar actions sit in the action bar.

03Journal New

Your digital field notebook.

Organise site visits, voltage readings and fault reports into notebooks, sections and pages — structured the way you think, with rich text editing powered by TipTap.

Notebooks → Sections → Pages

Three-level hierarchy mirrors how you organise work — one notebook per project, sections for areas, pages for individual visits.

Full rich text editor

Headings, bold, lists, tables, images, code blocks — a full TipTap editor so your field notes look professional.

Voice-to-table recorder

Say "24 cells, cell 1 is 12.4 volts" — the AI walks through each value and inserts a formatted table straight into your page.

Firestore cloud sync

Every page syncs in real time. Start a note on your phone, finish it on your tablet — always up to date.

Desktop context menus

Right-click any notebook, section or page to rename, recolour, change date, copy or move — no digging through menus.

04–06The rest of the week

Finder, Routines & Callout.

04 Finder

Search every exchange and cabinet.

Address, coordinates, power supply, security, emergency contacts. Separate tabs for exchanges and cabinets. Live Google Map on desktop.

05 Routines

A year of routine visits in one grid.

Sites as rows, months as columns. Visits logged in your timesheet are auto-detected and flagged with an AUTO badge. Stats, Due and Never-Visited tabs on mobile; a full dashboard on desktop.

06 Callout

On-call weeks and incidents, together.

Mark which weeks you're on-call or doing extra shifts. Log each fault against its week: time, site, description, actions taken. Paste a Remedy ticket and it parses the site, fault and date automatically. Callouts roll into the weekly email export.

07Desktop mode

A dedicated desktop layout.

When the screen is 1280 px or wider, Rian switches to purpose-built multi-panel views (navigation, list, detail) sharing data and state with the mobile app.

Three-panel timesheet

Days, Tasks and Detail side by side. Keyboard-driven.

Weekly summary sidebar

Percentage bars and ORD/OT columns. Weekend days auto-collapse when empty.

Every view reimagined

Notes, Callouts, Finder and Routines each have a desktop layout with wider editors and live Google Maps where useful.

Shared state with mobile

Collapse a section on the laptop, it's collapsed on the phone. Universal ESC closes whichever modal is open.

Launch the app.

Opens in a browser or installs as a PWA. Sign in with Google to sync across devices.
