01 Timesheet

Every hour logged against its code. In seconds.

Tap any day, drop in a task, pick a work code and your ordinary vs overtime hours — the app keeps the running total, highlights OT, and exports the week by email.

39hordinary +3overtime · this week

Week at a glance

All 7 days in one scroll. Task counts and hours inline so you see the shape of your week before you dig in.

Ordinary & overtime, separated

Each task has its own ORD and OT inputs. The summary adds it up the way payroll expects.

Codes that remember you

Search KLE · MXY0002A10 · bundles like "Routines" insert three codes at once. No fishing through a spreadsheet.

One-tap weekly email

Formatted summary with ORD / OT breakdown, codes and descriptions — sent from your phone, read-ready by your foreman.

02 Notes

A running record of everything that isn't time.

Faults, follow-ups, meetings, parts on order — captured with a category, a priority dot, and (if you need it) a reminder push notification.

Six categories, colour coded

Job · Fault · Follow-up · Meeting · Personal · Idea — every note is instantly recognisable from a glance at the list.

Filter by category & priority together

Pinch the list down to "All open High-priority Faults" with two taps. Active filters show as dismissible pills.

Reminders that actually fire

Set a date/time on any note. You get a push notification — even if the app is closed — at the moment it's due.

Dictate, search, bulk-mark

Hands full? Tap the mic. Got 40 old notes? Long-press to multi-select, mark done or bin in one go.
